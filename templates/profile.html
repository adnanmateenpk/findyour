What does the findyour Rating mean?

Our Number is rated using the following Points reward system:

  • 35 pts if the company is licensed and presents the relevant forms to the findyour team
  • 35 pts if the company has the relevant insurances and presents the relevant forms to the findyour team
  • 15 pts if ABN has been verified by the findyour team
  • 10 pts for pictures of previous work to attach to the profile (or a link to their previous work)
  • 5 pts for postitive customer feedback/testimonials before starting findyour
